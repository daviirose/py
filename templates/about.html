What It Takes

Disciplined

Being disciplined is one of the most important core values you could attain, showing control of your behavior when being told what to do and how to do it is very important. To simply have a routine and go by it can be crucial as well. Staying on top of work when you feel things are going downhill or even when you are having a bad day will set you apart from others not just in the work place but within your own personal life.

Perseverance

Ever since I was a little boy I played sports. I think playing team sports builds character but also gives you an insight to probem solving skills at an early age.

Humility

Being humble is a beautiful trait to have. Remember where you came from and those who helped you get to where you are. You yourself are unique, but you come from elsewhere, not from yourself. While in the work place, this is an important trait due to the fact that you will do great things but failure is also part of life. It's better to be humble at all times or failure will humble you in a heart beat.
